What is 3D Animation?

Unlike 2D animation which occurs on a flat plane (X and Y axis), 3D animation creates movement within a three-dimensional digital environment, utilizing the Z-axis for depth.

Z-Axis Depth

Adds volume, height and width, creating a navigable space.

Digital Mesh

Wireframe structures of polygons defining object shapes.

Rendering

Lighting, shadows, and physics processed by the computer.
