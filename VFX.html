Key Techniques & Tools

The building blocks of digital illusion.

Compositing

Combines multiple visual layers (live footage, CGI, effects) into a single seamless shot with realistic lighting and depth.

Keyframing

Defines motion and changes over time by setting start and end points for position, scale, rotation, opacity, or effects.

Particle Systems

Creates dynamic effects like smoke, fire, sparks, dust, and magic using thousands of animated particles.

Rotoscoping

Manually traces subjects frame by frame to isolate them for background replacement or visual effects.

3D Camera Tracking

Analyzes live-action footage to recreate camera movement in 3D space so CGI elements lock naturally into the scene

Kinetic Typography

Animates text using movement, timing, and rhythm to visually enhance storytelling and emotion.
